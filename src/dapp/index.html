Airline Management

In this region you could manage the creation, founding and voting of Airlines. Up to four airlines would be added without voting. From there, current airlines will vote to create a new one. Airlines could also fund the contract with 10 ether each.

Passenger Management

Is Operational:

In this region you could add passengers up to Five. Each passenger could purchase a flight from airlines that have funded 10 ether in Airline Management. Also, in this region, each passenger could withdraw any Ether gained as a result of delays in flight covered by the insurance. Finally the balance of each passenger is shown.
